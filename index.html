Welcome to Cosmere Interplanetary Travel Agency

Embark on the journey of a lifetime and explore the wonders of the Cosmere. Whether you're seeking adventure, tranquility, or mysterious enchantment, our carefully curated destinations offer unforgettable experiences.

Featured Destinations

Scadrial: The Land of Mist and Metals

Venture into mist-covered Luthadel, where ash falls softly from the sky, noble houses scheme, and allomancers wield metal and mystery in a city pulsing with danger and allure

Roshar: Storms, Spren, and Splendor

Adventure awaits in this vibrant and tempestuous land. Visit Urithiru's majestic tower, trek the Shattered Plains, and immerse yourself in the wonders of spren-infused life.

Nalthis: Where Color is Magic

Awaken your senses in the land where every color has power. Explore Hallandren's colorful markets, witness vibrant celebrations, and bask on sun-drenched beaches that inspire artists and adventurers alike.

Sel: Mysteries of Ancient Magic

Delve into the rich history and powerful magic of Sel. Wander the glowing streets of Elantris, experience the courtly intrigue of Arelon, and discover the transformative magic of AonDor.
